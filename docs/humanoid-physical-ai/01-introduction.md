# 1. Introduction to Humanoid & Physical AI Robotics

Welcome to the fascinating world of humanoid and physical AI robotics. This field represents the convergence of advanced artificial intelligence with complex mechanical systems, aiming to create robots that can interact with the physical world in a human-like manner.

This book will guide you through the core concepts, from the evolution of humanoids to the intricacies of their design, perception, and control.
