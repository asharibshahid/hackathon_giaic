# 9. Real-World Applications

Humanoid robots are poised to revolutionize numerous industries.

- **Logistics and Warehousing:** Automating package handling and inventory management.
- **Healthcare:** Assisting with patient care and rehabilitation.
- **Exploration:** Operating in hazardous environments, from disaster sites to space.
- **Entertainment and Personal Assistance:** Providing companionship and help in daily life.
