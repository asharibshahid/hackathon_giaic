# 2. The Evolution of Humanoid Robots

The concept of automata and human-like machines dates back centuries, but the modern era of humanoid robotics began in the latter half of the 20th century.

- **Early Stages:** Focused on basic locomotion and simple tasks.
- **21st Century:** Rapid advancements in AI, sensors, and materials have led to more agile and intelligent robots.
- **Current State:** Robots capable of complex manipulation, dynamic balancing, and learning from their environment.
