# 10. Safety, Ethics, and the Future

As humanoid robots become more capable, it is crucial to address the associated safety and ethical considerations.

- **Safety Standards:** Developing robust protocols to prevent accidents.
- **Ethical Frameworks:** Addressing questions of autonomy, accountability, and the impact on employment.
- **The Future:** The continued integration of AI and robotics promises a future where humanoids are an integral part of society, augmenting human capabilities and tackling global challenges.
