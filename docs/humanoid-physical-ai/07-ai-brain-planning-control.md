# 7. The AI Brain: Planning and Control

The AI is the robot's "brain," responsible for decision-making, planning, and controlling the body.

- **Planning:** Algorithms for pathfinding, task scheduling, and motion planning.
- **Control:** Real-time adjustments to maintain balance, execute movements, and interact with objects.
- **Machine Learning:** Reinforcement learning and imitation learning are used to train robots for complex tasks.
